About me!

I graduated from the University of Calgary in December 2021 with a degree in Mechanical Engineering and a specialization in Energy and Environmental Engineering. In my second year, I started this specialization knowing that I would like to focus my career on the low carbon energy industry. Through this specialization, I enrolled in several courses related to energy including gas dynamics, aerodynamics, and turbomachinery.

During my time at the U of C, I was a member of the Zeus Electric Motorsport team. In this club, we designed, built, and raced a fully electric motorcycle. Through this team, I gained experience in design, prototyping, and testing.

When I'm not working, you can find me biking, running, or playing guitar. In 2021, I took my bicycle from Calgary to Vancouver (you can read about the trip here! ). After this trip, I fell in love with bike touring and in 2024, biked from Banff to the Mexico border on the GDMBR.
