Biking the GDMBR!

The idea for this trip was planted in my mind around 4 years ago in 2020 after watching Ryan Van Duzer's video of the trail. I put the trip on my bucket list but didn't expect to be able to do it so soon! I wanted at least two months to complete the trip so that I wouldn't feel rushed, so being between jobs made the timing perfect.
